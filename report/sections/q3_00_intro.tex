\subsection*{Scenario and domain}

The assigned domain is public transport operated by ASEAG and Deutsche Bahn
in the context of the \emph{2036 Olympic Games in Aachen}, where a surge in
passenger demand requires coordinated shuttle and train services for athletes,
officials, and spectators travelling between Olympic venues.
The simulated process covers the full journey lifecycle: group reservations are
created, individual tickets are issued, passengers are assigned to a departure
batch, each passenger boards via a channel determined by their accreditation
status, the departure leaves with a synchronized batch of passengers, and every
passenger individually completes their journey.
The OCEL\,2.0 event log is submitted as \texttt{data/log.sqlite} (SQLite
serialization) alongside an optional JSON mirror at \texttt{data/log.json}.
